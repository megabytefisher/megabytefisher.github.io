About Me

Welcome to kev.fish!

My name is Kevin Fisher, and this is my personal website. I am a software developer in Memphis, TN.

My Story

When I was about 11 (around 2006), I wanted to make a video game. I eventually found a free 2D tile-based game engine that allowed my friends and I to create our own little worlds and characters.

We loved playing around and learning how to do things in the engine, but then we discovered that the engine's forums contained guides and tutorials to add entirely new features to it.

Stumbling through those Visual Basic 6 tutorials was my first exposure to programming, and I haven't stopped since. Over the next few years and into high school, I became very interested in reverse engineering and modding games, and I learned C, C++, x86/64 assembly.

Nowadays, I mainly develop in C#. I love to learn new technologies and experiment with hardware hacking in my free time. I also love debugging complex issues that can take hours to solve.
